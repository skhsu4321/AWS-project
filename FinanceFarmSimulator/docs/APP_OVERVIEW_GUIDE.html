🌱 Finance Farm Simulator

Gamified Personal Finance Management App

🎯 What is Finance Farm Simulator?

Finance Farm Simulator transforms boring financial management into an engaging farm simulation game. Users track their income, expenses, and savings goals while watching their virtual farm grow and prosper based on their real financial progress.

🌾 How It Works

💰

Income = Fertilizer

Every time you log income, your crops get fertilizer and grow faster. Build income streaks for bonus growth!

🧮 Technical Implementation

Growth Formula:

cropGrowth = (incomeAmount / goalTarget) * 0.1 * streakMultiplier

Streak Bonus:

streakMultiplier = 1 + (streakDays * 0.05) // Max 2.0x

Example: $500 income toward $5000 goal with 10-day streak = 1.5% growth

Files: src/components/income/FertilizerAnimation.tsx

🌱

Savings = Crops

Your savings goals become virtual crops. The more you save, the more your crops grow and flourish.

🧮 Technical Implementation

Progress Calculation:

progressPercent = (currentAmount / targetAmount) * 100

Crop Stages:

  • Seed: 0-25% progress
  • Sprout: 25-50% progress
  • Growing: 50-75% progress
  • Mature: 75-99% progress
  • Harvest: 100% progress

Files: src/components/goals/GoalProgressBar.tsx

🌿

Expenses = Weeds

Expenses appear as weeds in your farm. Pull them out by categorizing and managing your spending.

🧮 Technical Implementation

Weed Density Formula:

weedCount = Math.floor((totalExpenses / monthlyIncome) * 10)

Budget Alert Thresholds:

  • Yellow Warning: spent ≥ budget * 0.8 (80%)
  • Orange Alert: spent ≥ budget * 0.9 (90%)
  • Red Critical: spent ≥ budget * 1.0 (100%)

Files: src/components/expenses/WeedPullingInterface.tsx

🏆

Goals = Harvest

Complete financial goals to harvest your crops and unlock new farm areas and features.

🧮 Technical Implementation

Time Estimation:

daysRemaining = Math.ceil((targetAmount - currentAmount) / avgDailyIncome)

On-Track Calculation:

onTrack = (currentAmount / daysElapsed) >= (targetAmount / totalDays)

Harvest Animation: Triggers when progress reaches 100%

Files: src/components/goals/GoalCompletionModal.tsx

🧮 Mathematical Functions & Algorithms

Farm Growth Calculation

Formula: cropGrowth = (incomeAmount / goalTarget) * baseGrowthRate * streakMultiplier

  • baseGrowthRate: 0.1 (10% per income entry)
  • streakMultiplier: 1 + (streakDays * 0.05) // Max 2.0x
  • Example: $500 income toward $5000 goal with 10-day streak = 1.5% growth
Budget Alert System
  • Yellow Warning: spent ≥ budget * 0.8 (80%)
  • Orange Alert: spent ≥ budget * 0.9 (90%)
  • Red Critical: spent ≥ budget * 1.0 (100%)
  • Weed Formula: weedCount = Math.floor((totalExpenses / monthlyIncome) * 10)
Goal Progress Tracking
  • Progress: (currentAmount / targetAmount) * 100
  • Days Remaining: Math.ceil((target - current) / avgDailyIncome)
  • Crop Stages: Seed (0-25%), Sprout (25-50%), Growing (50-75%), Mature (75-99%), Harvest (100%)

📊 Chart & Graph Implementation

Income vs Expense Chart
  • Type: Line Chart with dual Y-axis
  • Library: react-native-chart-kit
  • Data: Daily/Weekly/Monthly aggregates
  • Colors: Income (#4CAF50), Expenses (#F44336)
Spending Category Breakdown
  • Type: Pie Chart with percentages
  • Categories: Food, Transport, Entertainment, Bills, Other
  • Formula: categoryPercent = (categoryTotal / totalExpenses) * 100
Savings Trend Analysis
  • Type: Area Chart with trend line
  • Formula: savings = totalIncome - totalExpenses
  • Projection: Linear regression extends 90 days into future
